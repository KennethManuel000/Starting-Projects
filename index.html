Introduction

This report is a comprehensive record of the calls handled by our agents, broken down on a daily basis for the entire month. It provides an in-depth view of our operations, with metrics such as Average Handling Time (AHT) being part of the report. The importance of this report goes beyond providing operational insights, as it also serves as the foundation for our monthly invoicing process. By accurately documenting the work done by our agents, it ensures transparency and fairness in our billing process. Hence, the ‘Billable Report’ stands as a symbol of our commitment to accountability and operational excellence.

How to dowload data
  1. You can download the data by accessing this link: LGECI AWS
    • Please utilize the login credentials that were provided to you.
  2. Once you're in, you'll see navigation icons on the left side of your screen. Hover your mouse over the second icon, which is labeled as “Analytics and Optimization”. Within this menu, you'll find an option that says “Dashboards and Reports”. Go ahead and click on it to access the “Dashboards and Reports” section.
  3. click on "Historical metrics" and look for "For Billable Hours Report Stafftime & PPM"
PPM
  1. Click on the gear Icon and choose the date you want to download.
  2. Click on actions dropdown and download your data.
  3. Note: You can only download 1 date at a time
  4. Once you've downloaded the data, save it in your raw data folder. (its up to your own preference on how you want to store your data)
AUX
  1. Click on the gear Icon and choose the date you want to download.
  2. Click on actions dropdown and download your data.
  3. Once you've downloaded the data, save it in your raw data folder. (its up to your own preference on how you want to store your data)
Updating the Report
  1. Open the Billable Report and start by updating PPM.
  2. Go to AWS PPM_r Sheet and paste your newly downloaded data for PPM and extend the columns that has Formula.
  3. Check for #N/A on the columns that has red font color.
  4. If you encounter any #N/As. Backtrack the formula and update the necessary data.
  5. Go to AWS PPM_c sheet and refresh the pivot table.
  6. Copy the data from column H to HA and past it to RawData_PPM sheet.
  7. Extend the formula from column AB to.
  8. Do the same steps for staff time by starting with the sheet AWS Aux_r.
  9. Refresh all pivot tables
